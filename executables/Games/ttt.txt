ttt - Tic-Tac-Toe

Play Tic-Tac-Toe against the computer.
You are X, the computer is O. Coin flip decides who goes first.

Controls:
  1-9           Place mark (numpad layout)
  Click         Place mark on cell
  Escape        Exit

Numpad layout:
  7 | 8 | 9
  ---------
  4 | 5 | 6
  ---------
  1 | 2 | 3

Usage: run the 'ttt' executable to play

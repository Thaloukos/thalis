tic_tac_toe - Tic-Tac-Toe

Play Tic-Tac-Toe against the computer.
You are X, the computer is O.
First player is either the last loser, or decided through a coin flip.

Controls:
  1-9           Place mark (numpad layout)
  Click         Place mark on cell
  Escape        Exit

See if you can't defeat the all powerful Tic-Tac-Toe AI!

Usage: run the 'tic_tac_toe' executable to play
